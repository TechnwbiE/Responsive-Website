Meet the Phantom Thieves

Get to know the masked vigilantes who challenge injustice in the shadows of Tokyo.

Joker

The player-controlled protagonist. Silent, strategic, and charismatic. Joker leads the Phantom Thieves and wields the Wild Card, allowing him to use multiple Personas.

Ryuji Sakamoto (Skull)

Loud, brash, but loyal. A former track star with a rebellious heart. His Persona is Captain Kidd.

Ann Takamaki (Panther)

Strong and compassionate. Ann balances her modeling career with fighting injustice. Her Persona is Carmen.

Morgana (Mona)

A mysterious, talking creature who serves as a guide in the Metaverse. His Persona is Zorro.

Yusuke Kitagawa (Fox)

A talented and eccentric artist. Deeply philosophical and dedicated to beauty. His Persona is Goemon.

Makoto Niijima (Queen)

Smart, composed, and fearless. Student council president turned rebel. Her Persona is Johanna.
